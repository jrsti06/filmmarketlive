Paparazzi Mistakes Jeff Ryan for Robert Pattinson — “Ugly Photos Clarified Ryan”

LOS ANGELES, CA — In a now-viral moment outside a screening of Mooch, local paparazzi swarmed indie filmmaker Jeff Ryan, mistakenly identifying him as Twilight star Robert Pattinson.

Wearing a hoodie, shades, and what can only be described as “post-ironic despair,” Ryan posed for the cameras before realizing the error.

“They kept yelling ‘Rob! ROB!’ and I thought… sure, maybe I am today,” said Ryan.

The truth unraveled moments later when a high-resolution flash revealed Ryan’s true identity — and the crowd slowly began to disperse.

“I just thought Pattinson had fallen on hard times,” one photographer admitted. “The light hit him wrong and it was like, ‘Wait, who the hell is this guy?’”

Ryan has since updated his IMDb headshot with one of the photos from the incident and is reportedly developing a short called “Walmart Pattinson: A Tragedy.”
