Paparazzi Mistake Jeff Ryan for Robert Pattinson — "Ugly Photos Clarified Identity," Say Sources

HOLLYWOOD, CA — A flash mob of paparazzi at a West Hollywood smoothie stand briefly caused a stir after misidentifying indie filmmaker Jeff Ryan as Twilight star Robert Pattinson.

The chaos erupted when Ryan exited a 2004 Toyota Prius in oversized sunglasses and a hoodie that read "Emo Forever." Cameras clicked. Voices screamed. "Rob! ROB!"

"I thought I was finally famous," Ryan said. "Turns out, they just hadn’t seen my chin clearly."

Within moments, a particularly high-resolution image of Ryan tripping over a sandwich board made its way to TMZ. The caption: “Definitely Not Pattinson.”

Ryan has since updated his IMDb bio to include the phrase: "Often confused for Robert Pattinson in dim light."
