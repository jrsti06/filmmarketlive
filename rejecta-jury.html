Rejecta Film Festival Announces Its First-Ever Jury

The inaugural Rejecta Film Festival has unveiled its full lineup of jury members — a group of cinematic disruptors, emo auteurs, and reluctant tastemakers brave enough to judge films rejected by everyone else.

🏆 Grand Jury Prize

Aaron Katz & Adam Maffei
For the film that made us feel the most things. Like, emotionally. Maybe physically? Hard to say.

🎖️ Special Jury Prize

Kyra Gardner, Addison Heimann, Elise Finnerty
For something we can’t quite explain — but loved anyway. The vibes were immaculate.

🎬 Best Director

Tristan Scott-Behrends
For bringing a bold vision to every painful, exhausting, beautiful day on set. You deserve an actual career. We mean that.

🎭 Best Lead Performance

Matt Perusse & Sarah J. Mann
(Gender-neutral category)
For the actors who *really went for it* — and made us believe it.

✍️ Best Screenplay

Jackson Murray
For writing that either felt improvised or brilliant (or both). Lines that live rent-free in our minds.

💡 Innovation + Pure Brilliance

Anonymous*
(*they didn’t want to be publicly associated with Jeff Ryan)
For ideas that defy logic and possibly legality. We love you anyway.

🔥 Spirit of Rejecta

Peter Nogueira
Awarded to the film that best embodies our festival’s core tenets: rebellion, DIY chaos, and a total lack of adult supervision.
